how it works

#connect

introduce yourself

who you are, what you're working on, what you offer, what you're looking for. one post, honest and brief.

#needs

post a need

one need per message. what it is, who it helps, what done looks like, your deadline and time zone.

#offers

post an offer

what you can do, who it's best for, your availability and limits. clear constraints are a gift to everyone.

#matches

pair and move

need meets offer. name an owner. name a next step. 48-hour moves. 2-week sprints. ship over debate.

tenets

this is a space that runs on mutuality.

honesty, consistency, repair, and respect for boundaries. if that sounds like a lot, it isn't — it's just basic relational hygiene. here's what that looks like in practice:

01

non-extractive

clear asks. offer something back or timebox. this is an exchange, not a favour bank.

02

consent first

no unsolicited DMs. ask in-channel before sliding into anyone's inbox.

03

one item per post

keep it scannable. one need, one offer, one idea. clarity is kindness.

04

ship over debate

propose, name an owner, set a deadline. progress beats perfect every time.

05

credit + permission

don't repost others' work outside drblx without asking. what's shared here stays here unless agreed otherwise.

06

no harassment or pile-ons

critique ideas, not people. mods enforce this without debate.

07

keep it public-safe

treat every post as if anyone could read it. personal details, private projects, sensitive data — keep those in DMs or off-platform.

08

no epistemic bullying

debate-as-domination — sealioning, gotchas, tone-policing — is not welcome here and will result in removal. critique the idea. respect the person.
